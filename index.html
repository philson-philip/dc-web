Import Export (IE) Code

IE Code is mandatorily required by all businesses which are engaged in the Import and Export of goods in India. It is a 10-digit code that is issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT), Government of India. This code requires a one-time registration process and lifetime validity. Our team will assist the business units to complete the process.

Trademark

Trademarks in India are registered under the Trademark Act, 1999, listed by the Controller General of Patents Designs and Trademarks, Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India. Trademark gives the owner the power to sue for losses when a violation of trademarks happens.
A Trademark is a unique logo or word name, symbol, phrase, mark that identifies the product or service of a particular provider. It is done to protect the trademark rights of a party and to prevent usage of the trademark by any other business thus, helping to preserve and protect the uniqueness and ownership rights of your brand or entity, customer recognition.
We will take care of your Trademark registration process.

MSME Registration

For promoting entrepreneurship, the government provides special benefits to small businesses in the form of subsidies and incentives. Also, banks give loans at concessional rates to set up these businesses. For availing those, the small businesses need to register under the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) act. Our team will collaborate with the business units to complete the process.

PAN

PAN stands for Permanent Account Number. A PAN number is a ten-digit number in alphabets and numerical, or in 'alphanumeric' terms, that is allocated by the income tax department to all the taxpayers, and is unique with each individual.

FSSAI

FSSAI license is granted by The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India to all food business operators in India. FSSAI license is mandatory before starting any food business.  All the food business operators must follow all the rules & regulations of FSSAI for food quality control. FSSAI Registration number will be a 14 digit number that needs to be printed on food packages. This license needs to be renewed and an annual return needs to be filed.
Our services extent to:

  • FSSAI Registration
  • FSSAI Central License
  • FSSAI State License
  • Annual Return filing and other related forms

Legal Metrology

Legal Metrology is a part of the Department of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution Wing of the Government of India regulating weights and measures. It deals with the monitoring and regulation of manufacturing, import, and business of weighing and measuring instruments and devices.
 The sale or distribution of all packaged goods in India such as export goods, food products, and consumer goods requires a Legal Metrology Certificate. Its objective is to ensure public guarantee from the point of view of accuracy and security of the weights and measurements. We will lend a hand to support you through the process.

Income Tax Filing

Income tax is a type of direct tax that the central government charges on the income earned during a financial year by the taxpayers including individuals. Failing to file income tax returns may attract penal provisions under the Income Tax Act,1961. 
The Income tax Act has classified the types of taxpayers in categories so as to apply different tax rates for different types of taxpayers.
Taxpayers are categorized as below:

  • Individuals, Hindu Undivided Family (HUF), Association of Persons (AOP) and Body of Individuals (BOI)
  • Firms
  • Companies

Our team will collaborate with you on filing your income tax returns on time after considering and evaluating the income, exempted income details, expenses, allowable deductions under the law. >

Tax Deduction Account Number (TAN)

Tax Deduction Account Number or Tax Collection Account Number is a 10 -digit alpha- numeric number issued by the Income-tax Department. TAN is to be obtained by all persons who are responsible for deducting tax at source (TDS) or who are required to collect tax at source (TCS).
We will take care of your TAN registration process, the Quarterly filing of the TDS returns and providing you the professional advice for the monthly deductions/collections of tax under the various provisions of Income Tax Act,1961.

ESI / PF

Employees' State Insurance (ESI) is a self-financing social security and health insurance scheme for Indian workers. The fund is managed by the Employees' State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) according to rules and regulations stipulated in the ESI Act 1948.
Employees' Provident Fund is a statutory body established by the Employees' Provident Fund and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952. When an employee becomes an active member of the scheme, a 12 digit Universal Account Number (UAN) will be generated, which will act as an umbrella for the multiple Member Ids allotted to an individual by different establishments and also remains the same through the lifetime of an employee.
We will lend a hand to support the registration process of both PF & ESI and the monthly filing of the same.

Goods and Service Tax Act (GST)

The Goods and Service Tax Act (GST) was came into effect on 1st July 2017 which subsumed the erstwhile laws such as excise duty, service tax and VAT, etc. Tax will be levied on every supply of goods and services by both Centre and State Government. In a nutshell we shall say that GST is one indirect tax for the whole nation, which will make India one unified common market.
Our services extent to:

  • Registration, modification or amendment under GST Act
  • Filing of GST returns
  • Obtaining Letter of Undertaking (LUT) for export of services or goods
  • Consultation and advice on GST matters
